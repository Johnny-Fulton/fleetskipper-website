## NEW LAYOUT STRUCTURE

### Card Order:
1. **ALL CREW** - Universal Requirements (everyone onboard)
2. **MASTER** - Certificate of Competency + Role-Specific Training
3. **ADDITIONAL CREW** - Second Person / Deck Crew
4. **MINIMUM COVERAGE** - At least one person must have...
5. **ENGINEERING** - Conditional (only shows if required)
6. **ADDITIONAL TRAINING** - Conditional (radar, charts, stability, catering)

## EMBEDDED WBC3 TABLES - FULL CONTENT

### Table A5.1 - Master & Deck Manning
```markdown
## Table A5.1 — Master Qualifications & Deck Manning Requirements

### Master Qualifications by Area Category

| Area Category | Minimum Acceptable Master Qualifications |
|---------------|------------------------------------------|
| **Cat 0 - Unrestricted** | • Boatmaster T0<br>• MCA/RYA Yachtmaster Ocean<br>• STCW Master <3000GT<br>• STCW OOW <3000GT Unlimited |
| **Cat 1 - Up to 150nm** | • Boatmaster T1<br>• MCA/RYA Yachtmaster Offshore<br>• Higher levels accepted |
| **Cat 2 - Up to 60nm** | • Boatmaster T2<br>• MCA/RYA Yachtmaster Coastal<br>• Higher levels accepted |
| **Cat 3 - Up to 20nm** | • Boatmaster T3<br>• MCA/RYA Yachtmaster Coastal<br>• Higher levels accepted |
| **Cat 4, 5, 6 - Limited** | • Boatmaster T4<br>• RYA Powerboat Level 2<br>• RYA Day Skipper<br>• Higher levels accepted |

### Additional Manning Requirements (Second Person)

| Area Category | Additional Deck Crew Requirements |
|---------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Cat 0** | Another person holding at least Yachtmaster Offshore or STCW OOW |
| **Cat 1** | Second person holding at least RYA/MCA Yachtmaster Coastal |
| **Cat 2** | Second person deemed competent by master |
| **Cat 3, 4, 5, 6** | Second person that vessel owner/operator considers experienced and competent |

**Note 1:** Higher-level certificates are valid at lower categories (e.g., Boatmaster T2 is valid for T3).

**Note 2:** See WBC3 Appendix 5 for full details and alternative qualifications.
```

### Table A5.2 - Engineering Manning
```markdown
## Table A5.2 — Minimum Engineering Manning Requirements

| Engineering Qualification | Area Categories | Power/Vessel Type |
|--------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Approved Engine Course (Part 1) OR demonstrated competency | 6-3 | Power Vessel W<br>Power Vessel SL |
| | 2, 1, 0 | Power Vessel SL |
| MEOL or STCW III/4 Engine Ratings or AEC (Part 1&2) | 2, 1, 0 | Power Vessel W<br><1500 kW |
| SMEOL, STCW C/Eng (Y4) | 2, 1, 0 | Power Vessel W<br>≥1500 kW < 3000 kW |
| Small Vessel Second Engineer CoC | 2, 1, 0 | Power Vessel W<br>≥1500 kW < 3000 kW |
| STCW III/2 Small Vessel Chief Engineer <9000KW <3000GT | 0 | <3000GT |

**Note 2 (DUAL ROLE):** "The person holding the engineering requirement may be a crew member listed in Table A7.1."
**Translation:** Engineering can be the same person as Master or Second Person!

**Note 3 (EXPERIENCE EXEMPTION):** Persons who can demonstrate appropriate engineering experience and competency to MCA satisfaction may be granted exemption from Approved Engine Course.

**Note 5 (FAMILIARITY):** In all cases, one of the crew shall be sufficiently familiar with the operation and maintenance of the vessel's machinery to ensure safe passage.

**Note 6 (Power Vessel W):** Power Vessel employed in towing operations, lifting operations, or carriage of cargo greater than 1000 kg.

**Note 7 (Power Vessel SL):** Power Vessel other than Power Vessel W (standard operations).
```

## UI/UX DESIGN NOTES

### Visual Hierarchy:
1. **Card headers** - Large, bold, with emoji icons
2. **Requirements** - Checkmark bullets, clear requirement names
3. **Expandable buttons** - Chevron icons, clear "View/Hide" labels
4. **WBC3 Tables** - Bordered sections with "📋 WBC3 PROOF" header, light blue background

### Color Coding:
- **Universal requirements (All Crew)**: Green accents
- **Role-specific (Master/Second Person)**: Blue accents
- **Conditional (Engineering/Training)**: Amber/Warning accents
- **WBC3 Tables**: Blue-gray background with border-left accent

### Interactivity:
- All sections collapsible/expandable
- "View WBC3 Regulation" button expands individual regulation quotes
- "📋 WBC3 PROOF" button expands full tables
- Smooth animations for expansions

### Responsive:
- Cards stack vertically on mobile
- Tables become scrollable horizontally on small screens
- Maintain readability at all screen sizes
