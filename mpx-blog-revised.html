The Environmental and Safety Imperative

Beyond operational efficiency, paper MPX carries hidden environmental and safety costs:

Environmental Impact

  • Paper Production: Each ton of paper requires 24 trees and 7,000 gallons of water
  • Annual Waste: A mid-size port generates approximately 50,000 paper forms yearly
  • Carbon Footprint: Transportation, storage, and disposal add unnecessary emissions
  • ESG Compliance: Digital systems directly support sustainability reporting requirements

Safety Through Data

When the Ever Given blocked the Suez Canal in 2021, it disrupted $9.6 billion in trade daily. Post-incident analysis revealed that better data exchange and pattern recognition could have identified risk factors earlier. Paper forms trap this valuable intelligence in filing cabinets instead of feeding predictive safety systems.
