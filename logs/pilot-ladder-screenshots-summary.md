## Important Findings

### ✓ COMPLIANT Examples Found
The UKMPA interactive guide shows **ONLY compliant configurations**. All three scenarios demonstrate the CORRECT way to rig pilot transfer arrangements according to SOLAS V/23 regulations.

### ✗ NON-COMPLIANT Examples NOT Found
The interactive guide does not show non-compliant or incorrect examples. It is designed as a positive reference showing what operators SHOULD do, not what they should avoid.

### Regulatory Context Captured
The screenshots also captured important regulatory information:
- **SOLAS V/23 Regulation** - IMO MSC 110 amendments (June 25, 2025)
- **Implementation dates:**
  - New installations: January 1, 2028
  - Existing installations on SOLAS ships: First survey after January 1, 2029
  - Non-SOLAS ships: January 1, 2030
- **Key safety note:** Pilot ladders must be secured at intermediate lengths by a device-type approved to prevent slippage of side ropes and ladder spreading excessively (less than 635mm)
