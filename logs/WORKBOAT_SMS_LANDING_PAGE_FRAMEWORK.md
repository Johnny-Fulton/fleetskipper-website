## CONTENT WRITING GUIDELINES

### Voice & Tone:
- **Direct, no-nonsense** - Maritime operators hate fluff
- **UK-specific** - Use "MCA" not "USCG", "workboat" not "utility vessel"
- **Jargon-aware** - We can use maritime terms (they'll understand)
- **Trust-building** - Emphasize mariner credentials repeatedly

### What to Avoid:
- Generic corporate speak ("solutions", "leverage", "synergy")
- Overpromising ("guaranteed", "100% pass rate")
- Condescension (they know their vessels better than us)
- USA-centric language (this is UK market only)

### What to Emphasize:
- December 2026 deadline (urgency)
- Built by Master Mariner + Marine Pilot (credibility)
- 2-4 week turnaround (speed)
- Vessel-specific (not templates)
- Fair pricing (transparency)
- Digital future (innovation)
