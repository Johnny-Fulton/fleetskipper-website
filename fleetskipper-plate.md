## Start here: the three bottlenecks

These are blocking other things. Settle them first and a lot unlocks.

1. **Pick and file the new company name.** Almost everything downstream (new email, Google Workspace migration, marketing assets, accountant onboarding, contracts) depends on this being done.
2. **Decide UK Ltd vs. Australian entity for the move.** Affects accountant search, tax, email migration, and how you operate after relocating. Worth a one-hour chat with an accountant before committing.
3. **Find an accountant who understands UK → Australia founder moves.** They'll help with #2 and keep the rename clean.
