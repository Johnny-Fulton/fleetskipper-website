## 10. SUMMARY OF KEY REGULATION PATHWAYS

### 10.1 For Category 6 (Restricted Coastal)
**Area:** Within 3 miles of land, 3 mile radius from departure, daylight, favourable weather
**Master Minimum:** RYA Powerboat Level 2 (for open boats <3GT), RYA Day Skipper, or higher
**Crew:** +1 experienced and competent person
**Life-Saving:** 2 lifebuoys (1 with line), lifejackets 100%, 2 red hand flares, 2 smoke signals
**Radio:** VHF DSC (fixed or portable), Channel 70 DSC watch
**Training:** Sea survival, first aid (1 person), fire fighting (1 person if <15m)

### 10.2 For Category 5 (Limited Coastal Extended)
**Area:** Within 3 miles of land, 3 mile radius, favourable weather (no daylight restriction)
**Master Minimum:** RYA Day Skipper or higher
**Crew:** +1 experienced and competent person
**Life-Saving:** 2 lifebuoys (1 with light, 1 with line), lifejackets 100%, TPAs 100%, 4 parachute flares, 6 red hand flares
**Radio:** VHF DSC, Channel 70 DSC watch, PLB recommended
**Training:** Sea survival, first aid, fire fighting, radar (if fitted)

### 10.3 For Category 3 (Coastal - 20 miles)
**Area:** Up to 20 miles from safe haven
**Master Minimum:** RYA Yachtmaster Coastal, RYA Powerboat Advanced, or higher
**Crew:** +1 experienced and competent person
**Life-Saving:** 2 lifebuoys (1 with light, 1 with line), lifejackets 100%, TPAs 100%, 4 parachute flares, 6 red hand flares, PERD recommended
**Radio:** VHF DSC, Channel 70 DSC watch
**Training:** Sea survival, first aid, fire fighting, radar (if fitted), stability (if SIB required)

### 10.4 For Category 2 (Near Coastal - 60 miles)
**Area:** Up to 60 miles from safe haven
**Master Minimum:** RYA Yachtmaster Coastal or MCA Boatmasters' Licence
**Crew:** +1 person deemed competent with vessel/conditions experience
**Life-Saving:** 2 lifebuoys (1 with light, 1 with line), lifejackets 100%, rearming kits 100%, TPAs 100%, 1 EPIRB, 1 SART, 4 parachute flares, 6 red hand flares, PERDs
**Radio:** VHF DSC, Channel 70 DSC watch, may require MF/satellite depending on GMDSS area
**Training:** Sea survival, first aid, fire fighting, radar, stability (if SIB required), ECS (if fitted)

### 10.5 For Category 0 (Unrestricted)
**Area:** Unrestricted worldwide service
**Master Minimum:** STCW Master (Workboat <500GT unlimited) or RYA Yachtmaster Ocean
**Crew:** +1 person with Yachtmaster Offshore or STCW Master qualification minimum
**Life-Saving:** Full SOLAS equipment including 2 EPIRBs, SARTs, 12 parachute flares, spray hoods
**Radio:** Full GMDSS equipment for area (may include MF/HF, satellite, NAVTEX)
**Training:** Full STCW or equivalent, Medical Care Certificate for Master
