## 📝 CONTENT VOICE & PRINCIPLES

**DO:**
- Use plain English ("We'll build your SMS")
- Speak to practical operators ("This takes 2 hours")
- Show real maritime experience (specific examples)
- Be honest about DIY vs consultancy
- Use concrete numbers ("18-day turnaround")

**DON'T:**
- Corporate jargon ("solutions," "leverage")
- Oversell ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Force app mentions where not relevant
- Use stock maritime imagery (we have real photos!)
- Write like a lawyer

## 📞 DECISION LOG

**Decision 1: Navigation Structure** (Dec 9, 2025)
- **Decision:** Consultancy | App Waitlist | Compliance Guides | About | Contact
- **Reasoning:** Balances immediate revenue (consultancy) with future revenue (app), fixes user-reported "Resources messy" issue
- **Approved By:** User + 3 specialist teams (Marketing, UX, Content)

**Decision 2: Phased Approach** (Dec 9, 2025)
- **Decision:** Framework → Design → Content (not all at once)
- **Reasoning:** User preference for "get the framework, then design, then populate"
- **Approved By:** User

**Decision 3: Resources → Compliance Guides** (Dec 9, 2025)
- **Decision:** Rename Resources to Compliance Guides
- **Reasoning:** More specific, professional, SEO-friendly
- **Approved By:** Content team + User

**Decision 4: Consultancy-First Strategy** (Dec 9, 2025)
- **Decision:** Keep consultancy as primary until app launches
- **Reasoning:** Revenue NOW, build waitlist in parallel, smooth transition at launch
- **Approved By:** Marketing team + User
