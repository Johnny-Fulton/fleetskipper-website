   - `#0891B2` is literally ocean/sea color

2. **Better Energy & Modernity**
   - You're a NEW company (founded recently)

   - The bright cyan says "we're modern, we're different, we're digital-first"

3. **Better Contrast & Readability**
   - `#0891B2` pops more on white backgrounds
   - Easier to spot eyebrows and section markers
   - Better accessibility

4. **Easier to Maintain**
   - Tailwind's `cyan-600` or `cyan-500`
   - No custom hex values to remember
   - Consistent across design tools

5. **Actually Works Better with Your Orange CTA**
   - `#0891B2` (cool cyan) + `#c65d00` (warm orange) = great complementary contrast
   - `#4a5f7a` (cool navy) + `#c65d00` (warm orange) = okay but less pop
