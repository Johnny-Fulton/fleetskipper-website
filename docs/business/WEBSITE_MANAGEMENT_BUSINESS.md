## 🚨 EMERGENCY RESPONSE PROTOCOL

### Severity 1: CRITICAL (Site Down)
- **Response Time:** Immediate
- **Action:** Deploy rollback, investigate, fix
- **Notification:** Immediate alert to client

### Severity 2: HIGH (Major Bug)
- **Response Time:** Within 4 hours
- **Action:** Assess impact, deploy hotfix
- **Notification:** Same-day update to client

### Severity 3: MEDIUM (Minor Bug)
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours
- **Action:** Log issue, schedule fix
- **Notification:** Include in weekly report

### Severity 4: LOW (Enhancement)
- **Response Time:** Next sprint
- **Action:** Add to backlog
- **Notification:** Monthly review
