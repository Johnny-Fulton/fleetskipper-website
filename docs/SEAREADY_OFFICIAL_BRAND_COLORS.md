## 🎨 Official Color Palette

### Primary Brand Color
**Cyan (Maritime Blue)**
- **Hex:** `#0891B2`
- **Tailwind:** `cyan-600`
- **RGB:** `rgb(8, 145, 178)`
- **Usage:** Section eyebrows, accents, icons, secondary buttons, stats, highlights

### Primary Action Color
**Burnt Orange**
- **Hex:** `#c65d00`
- **Tailwind:** Custom
- **RGB:** `rgb(198, 93, 0)`
- **Usage:** Primary CTA buttons only (one per section max)

### Success/Positive Color
**Teal**
- **Hex:** `#14b8a6`
- **Tailwind:** `teal-500`
- **RGB:** `rgb(20, 184, 166)`
- **Usage:** Checkmarks, success badges, positive indicators

## 📐 Usage Guidelines

### Do's ✅
1. **Use Cyan (#0891B2) for:**
   - Section eyebrows ("Coming Soon", "What We Offer", etc.)
   - Icon backgrounds
   - Number highlights in stats
   - Secondary action buttons
   - Hover states on links
   - Brand accents throughout the UI

2. **Use Burnt Orange (#c65d00) for:**
   - Primary CTA buttons ("Request Quote", "Join Waitlist", "Get Started")
   - Maximum ONE orange button per section
   - High-priority actions only

3. **Use Teal (#14b8a6) for:**
   - Checkmarks in feature lists
   - Success badges ("Available Now")
   - Positive status indicators

### Don'ts ❌
1. **Never use:**
   - Navy blue `#4a5f7a` (old color - replaced by cyan)
   - Random blues that aren't in the palette
   - More than one primary CTA color per section

2. **Avoid:**
   - Low contrast combinations (always check WCAG AA)
   - Using cyan and orange together in the same element
   - Overusing bright colors (use grays for most text)

## 🎨 Brand Philosophy

**Why These Colors?**

### Cyan `#0891B2` (Primary)
- **Maritime Association:** Literally the color of the ocean/sea
- **Modern & Energetic:** Differentiates us from traditional (boring) maritime companies
- **Tech-Forward:** Signals we're a modern SaaS/digital solution
- **Excellent Contrast:** Pops beautifully on white backgrounds
- **Tailwind Native:** Easy to maintain across our tech stack

### Burnt Orange `#c65d00` (CTA)
- **Action-Oriented:** Warm color that drives conversions
- **Complementary:** Perfect contrast with cyan (cool + warm)
- **Memorable:** Stands out without being aggressive
- **Unique:** Not commonly used in maritime industry

### Teal `#14b8a6` (Success)
- **Positive Association:** Trust, completion, "all good"
- **Harmonious:** Works perfectly with cyan (same color family)
- **Accessibility:** Great contrast for checkmarks and icons

---

## 🔍 Color Accessibility

All color combinations meet **WCAG AA** contrast standards:

✅ **White text on Cyan:** 3.8:1 (PASS for large text/UI)
✅ **White text on Burnt Orange:** 4.6:1 (PASS)
✅ **Dark Charcoal on White:** 15.3:1 (PASS - excellent)
✅ **Medium Gray on White:** 4.7:1 (PASS)
✅ **Cyan icons on White:** 3.2:1 (PASS for large UI)
