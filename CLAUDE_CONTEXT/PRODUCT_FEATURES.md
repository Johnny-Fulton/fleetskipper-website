## 📦 PRODUCT LINEUP

### 1. SafeDeckOS Suite (Complete Vessel Compliance System)

**What It Is:**
Complete digital SMS management system with TWO integrated apps:

#### SafeDeckOS | Ops (Vessel Operations App)
**For:** Crew on vessels (deck-side)

**Purpose:** Daily vessel operations management

**Core Features:**
- 👥 **Crew Management**
  - Multi-vessel crew assignments
  - Certificate tracking with expiry warnings
  - Hours of Rest compliance (MGN 505)
  - Monthly grid view matching traditional HOR books
  - Automatic compliance calculations (10h/day, 77h/week)
  - Digital signatures for crew and authorized persons
  - Training & familiarisation tracking

- 🔧 **Maintenance Management**
  - 107+ pre-configured maintenance items
  - Automatic scheduling based on regulations
  - Equipment categories: LSA, FFE, Navigation, Radio, Machinery, Electrical, Medical
  - Job card system with parts tracking
  - Photo documentation capability

- 🚨 **Safety & Emergency**
  - Comprehensive drill library (fire, MOB, abandon ship, pollution)
  - Defect reporting with priority tracking
  - Risk assessment framework
  - Incident management with investigation workflow
  - Near-miss reporting

- 📊 **Compliance Dashboard**
  - Real-time compliance tracking
  - Color-coded status indicators
  - MCA inspection readiness
  - Monthly summaries
  - Automated reminders

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Zero setup time** - Pre-configured for UK operations
2. **Regulatory intelligence** - System knows EXACTLY what applies to YOUR vessel
3. **Offline-first** - Works without internet, syncs automatically
4. **UK-specific** - Not generic international software
5. **Built by mariners** - By a pilot and master mariner

---

#### SafeDeckOS | Audit (Shore-Side Compliance Management)
**For:** DPAs, shore management, compliance officers

**Purpose:** SMS documentation management, verification tracking, audit preparation

**Core Features:**
- 📋 **Document Management**
  - SMS policy documentation
  - Procedure library
  - Version control
  - Document review scheduling

- ✅ **Verification Tracking**
  - Track outstanding verifications
  - Close out observations
  - Manage deficiencies
  - Audit trail of all changes

- 🔍 **Audit Preparation**
  - Pre-inspection checklists
  - Gap analysis
  - Compliance reporting
  - Evidence gathering

- 📊 **Shore Oversight**
  - Fleet-wide compliance view
  - KPI dashboards
  - Trend analysis
  - Management reports

**Previously Known As:** SeaReady Office

### 2. SafeDeckOS | Pilot (Electronic Master/Pilot Exchange)

**What It Is:**
Digital pilot exchange system for modern ports and pilot organizations

**For:** Pilot organizations, harbour authorities

**Purpose:** Replace paper Master/Pilot Exchange forms with digital system

**Core Features:**
- 📱 **Digital Exchange**
  - Touch-optimized pilot exchange forms
  - Digital signatures (Master + Pilot)
  - Photo documentation capability
  - Offline operation

- 🔗 **PMIS Integration**
  - Direct integration with harbour PMIS systems
  - Real-time data sync
  - Job assignment integration
  - Automated data flow

- 📊 **Analytics & Reporting**
  - Exchange analytics
  - Pilot performance insights
  - Vessel statistics
  - Trend analysis

- 🔒 **Audit Trail**
  - Complete history of all exchanges
  - Tamper-proof records
  - Regulatory compliance (PMSC)
